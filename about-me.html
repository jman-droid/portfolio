My name is Josh Manley...

I'm a Seattle-based video creator and web developer.With over 5 years video experience, I am adept in all stages of production, but specialize in video editing and post-production coordination. My early exposure to video production at Sikh Coalition, a New York based civil rights non-profit, set the tone for my career. One of the first projects I produced was an anti-bullying campaign video for Sikh students and families. Since then, I have worked extensively with non-profit organizations, grassroots campaigns, and independent artists creating videos for social good. Recently, I assisted Sherae Lascelles, local social justice activist running for Washington’s 43rd LD House Rep. Position 2. Working with their video team, I helped craft a video from existing footage that reflected Sherae’s values and philosophy as an intersectional, marginalized, community-centric candidate. My work has always centered people.
